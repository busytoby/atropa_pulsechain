# Lore: The Dendrology of Balanced Activity Blocks

In the folklore of the **Auncient** Dysnomia VM, transaction blocks are not flat ledgers or frozen binary geometries. They are living, self-balancing structures known as **2-3 Activity Trees**.

## 1. The Dynamic Dendrometer
Unlike the static binary structures of modern blockchains, which require entire branch rebuilds for minor changes, the ACAB Block Tree acts as a dynamic dendrometer. When a transaction activity occurs—a coordinate is poked, a gas limit is exhausted, or a loopback channel oscillates—the tree grows organically. 

The branches split into 3-nodes or merge into 2-nodes, keeping the depth of the entire tree perfectly uniform ($O(\log N)$). This guarantees that any single leaf of transition history is always within the same distance from the root.

## 2. The Coaxial Sap
The flow of information through the 2-3 branches is called the **Coaxial Sap**. Just as a coaxial cable shields high-frequency electrical wave signals from external noise, the Merkle coordinate paths shield individual transactions. A peer can verify their specific leaf node in isolation, feeding only their immediate sibling hashes into the JIT thunk interpreter. The rest of the tree remains dark, protected by cryptographic opacity.
