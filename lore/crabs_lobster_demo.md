# Crabs & Lobster Drum & Bass Demo

A 2-minute microtonal chiptune video demo featuring coordinate vectors, scrolling notes, and coordinated crustacean animations.

- **The Lead Lobster**: The lead melody synthesizer performer is rendered as a majestic red lobster with an elongated segmented tail, fan flaps, 8 jointed walking legs, and larger claws.
- **Flocking & Proprioceptive Steering**: The crabs and lobster query adjacent locations, applying steering repulsion vectors to gracefully avoid overlapping and coordinate boundaries.
- **Drummer Formations**: Drummer performers hold intentional configurations aligned with their specific snare, bass, and hi-hat kits.
