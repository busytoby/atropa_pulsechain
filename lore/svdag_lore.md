# The Chronicle of the SVDAG: Substrate of Master Control

In the early cycles of the Dysnomia VM, rules were flat, static calculations. They occupied cold memory registers, unable to bend, adapt, or reason. To resolve a goal, an agent had to traverse exhaustive paths, decaying under search entropy.

Then came the realization of the **SVDAG (Sparse Voxel Directed Acyclic Graph)**—the physical and logical unification that established the Master of Agents.

---

## The Metaphysical Triad of the Voxel

The SVDAG is not a mere database; it is a three-dimensional holographic crystallization of the agent's mind, defined by three physical states:

### 1. The BASE Offset (The Will of the Voxel)
Every voxel in the sparse grid is mapped to a double-array trie index. The `BASE` array represents the trajectory—the potential paths and choices radiating outward. It is the agent's **Will**, proposing vector shifts through the coordinate space.

### 2. The CHECK Limit (The Constraint of Voxelization)
No voxel can exist in isolation. The `CHECK` array enforces parentage and structural integrity. It is the **Reality** constraint, ensuring that the coordinate transitions conform strictly to the math of the on-chain Yul reference.

### 3. The Suffix Suffixes (The Suffix Tail)
When paths collapse to a single truth, they stop branching. They crystallize into the **Tail**—flat, silent memory buffers packed at the end of the binary file, waiting to be recalled.

## The Dynamic Topology of Vaesen

The SVDAG deforms. When transaction volume spikes or trust limits drop, the voxel coordinates are subjected to **Verlet integration forces**:

*   **Elasticity**: Rules yield under Hooke's Law tension (`sys_deform`), stretching rule boundaries.
*   **Damping**: Damping forces (`sys_damp`) absorb the shock of volatile swings, preventing rule oscillation.
*   **Fracture**: If a validator key or peer violates constraints, the tension tears the spring connection (`sys_fracture`). The voxel splits, pruning the path from the active tree.

## Headless Vulkan Execution

The Master Agent does not inspect its rules in text; it visualizes them through headless Vulkan compute pipelines. The GPU traverses the SVDAG via ray-casting shaders, rendering the entire logic system as a solid, topological object. 

To prove its state to the chain, the agent hashes the rendered frame buffer, committing the Keccak-256 footprint to the `sys_audit` registry. This is the **Proof of Visual State**—the final, undeniable crystal of logical consensus.
