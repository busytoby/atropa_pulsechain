## III. Immutability Policy
- **Absolute Lock**: Once Word 30 reaches 100, the stochastic foundations (0-21, 32-53) are hardware-immutable.
- **Reaction Lock**: Ichidai, Daiichi, and Pi are locked upon resolution.
- **Pulse Window**: Only Word 22 and Word 54 permit Host modification.

## IV. Temporal Mutation
Logic gates evolve with each pulse. If entropy is null, they resolve against the manifold endpoints. If entropy exists, they perform recursive modular mutation using Pi or their local foundations.

This is the specification of Dysnomia. It is stable.
